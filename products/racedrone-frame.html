Racing Drone Frame Kit

The Crazepony racing quadcopter frames are designed to maximize speed,agility,and most of all fun. The frames utilize high quality full carbon fiber that is precision machined for a superb fit-and-finish.

H180 Airframe

Newest Smallest FPV airframe to date

The Crazepony H180 racing quadcopter frame is the smallest FPV airframe to date.Compared with the H250 or H180 frame,it is designed to maximize speed,agility,and most of all fun. The H180 utilizes high quality 3K carbon fiber that is precision machined for a superb fit-and-finish.

Feature

  • Full 3K Carbon Fiber airframe featuring a solid 3mm thick "Unibody" main plate.
  • Multiple motor mounting holes to fit a variety of motors (M3 and M2 screw mounting patterns).
  • "Bumpers" on the end of the arms to protect the motor during a crash, just like the QAV250.
  • Airframe weight 90g with included Landing Gear.

Suggested Build Parts

  • Props: Gemfan 5x3
  • Motors: Lumenier FX-2206 2000kv or similar
  • ESCs: 12amp ESCs with SimonK
  • Lipo: Lumenier 1300mAh 3s
  • FC: OpenPilot CC3D

H280/H250 Airframe

Most classic and popular FPV frame

The Crazepony H250/H280 airframe are the most classic FPV frame now.The H250 is a symmetric 250mm size airframe that accommodates 5" props. The 250mm is measured motor shaft to motor shaft diagonally.The H280 is up to 280mm and suit for both 5" and 6" props.The frame utilizes high quality 3K carbon fiber that is precision machined for a superb fit-and-finish.These are designed to support a 3s/4s lipo setup and powerful 1806/2204/2206 motors.
